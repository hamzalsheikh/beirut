Tabbouleh is a salad made of bulgur, tomatoes, onions, and parsley. It’s mixed with olive oil, lemon juice, and salt. It is served with lettuce on the side. Fattoush is a traditional Lebanese salad. Instead of croutons, we bake or fry pita bread and then crush it up and mix it in. The dressing is a mixture of olive juice, garlic, lemon, and salt.

Halawet Al Jubun is not Greek — it’s Lebanese. What is it? Halawit Al Jubun (cheese dessert) is a pastry made from layers of Cheese, lebanese cream(ashta), and assorted nuts and flower essance. The Lebanese people think of Halawet Al Jubun as gold. When I travel to Lebanon, I bring empty suitcases only to fill with boxes of Halawet Al Jubun to bring back home.
